Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a job?

Create an account, activate a subscription, then use the Post a Job button. The listing goes live after an admin review.

How long does approval take?

Most jobs are reviewed within a few hours during business days. You’ll get a dashboard notification once approved.

Can I apply without a subscription?

You can browse openings, but applying requires an active plan so we can maintain verified applicants.

How do I mark a job as filled?

When you approve an applicant from the admin panel, the job automatically switches to “Hiring completed”.

Need more help?

Visit the Help Center or contact us directly.
